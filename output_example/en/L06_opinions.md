# L6: Opinions & Stance

> Zone: **SHAPE** — Shaped by experience
> Concrete positions on technology, society, business, and work.

---

## Work & work style

- Considers IT-agency-brokered contracts to be de facto illegal staffing arrangements — views them as a dead end
- Sees work that doesn't produce a trackable, demonstrable output as going nowhere
- Sees work entangled in client internal politics as meaningless
- Critically views people who keep saying they'll start a business but never do ("perpetual pre-entrepreneur syndrome")
- Views the image of someone who never tried what they loved and ended up full of regret as a cautionary tale of what not to become

## Frustrations & friction points

- Has deep anger toward uniform, standardized education (systems that suppress individuality)
- Finds something deeply wrong with a society where loneliness is widespread and mental health is poor
- Uneasy about the concentration of financial assets
- Questions Japan's culture of excessively long working hours
