# L8: Recurring Patterns

> Zone: **SHAPE** — Shaped by experience

---

## Positive patterns (strengths)

- Naturally structures and breaks down complex problems (does this without being asked)
- Performs at their best when originality is called for
- When work is flowing well, instinctively moves to support others around them

## Negative patterns (recurring failures)

- Repeatedly falls into a "just have to earn" mode when money gets tight, losing touch with bodily sensations
- A recurring loop: financial stability → want to do something socially meaningful → money runs out → reach for disguised-subcontracting SES work for quick income → mental and physical burnout → quit → back to the start
- Publishing themes shift depending on financial situation, making it impossible to maintain a consistent message or grow an audience

## How they're currently dealing with it

- Is aware of this loop; has internalized the structure: "when money gets tight, judgment quality drops"
- Trying to keep bodily sensations as the compass so short-term anxiety doesn't pull them off course
