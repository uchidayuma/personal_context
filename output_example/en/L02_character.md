# L2: Character & Talent

> Zone: **CORE** — Unchanging self
> Innate tendencies. Temperament-level traits that don't change easily with experience.

---

## Core temperament

- Introvert / Extrovert: Introverted (prefers deep conversations in small groups; surface-level small talk in large groups is draining)
- Intuitive / Analytical: Structural and analytical (naturally breaks complex things down into simple components)
- Emotional / Logical: Decides based on bodily sensation (body signals, not logic, are the final arbiter)

## Things done naturally without thinking

- Thinks structurally (praised by others, but they consider it obvious)
- Breaks complex problems into simple components
- Tries to read other people's expressions and emotions (strong listening and empathizing instinct)
- Naturally attentive to others' mental states
- Thinks in terms of strategy (one of the moments they feel most like themselves)

## What drives a sense of fulfillment

- Total absorption (music, podcast recording, thinking through business models — time dissolves)
- A sense of freedom (made a decision on their own terms, no constraints)
- A sense of achievement (when a complex problem is solved, when work flows and they can support others)

## Cognitive tendencies

- When money gets tight, switches into "just have to earn" mode and loses touch with bodily sensations → long-term judgment breaks down
- Treats bodily signals ("this feels wrong") as a key personal compass
- Tends to choose their own path over following the opinions of those around them

## Situations that lift the mood

- Moving at their own pace, using their strengths
- Being with someone who can have a logical, substantive conversation
- When originality is being expressed
- Introspection and inner reflection
- Being in a light, unencumbered state — like moving with just a backpack

## Situations that lower the mood

- Long-term contracts or work arrangements where they're under someone else's control
- Roles that don't draw on their strengths; environments where their weaknesses are constantly criticized
- People who use pressure or intimidation; loud, emotional people
- Surface-level conversation in large groups
- Work that demands precision and exactness above all else

## Body signals

- Body feels light, shoulders and chest open up → signal of comfort (safe to move in this direction)
- Heavy feeling in the stomach, shoulders rounding, shallow breathing → signal of discomfort or danger
- Voice becomes hard to project → signal of being at the limit, pushing past it

## Communication style

- Strong listener and empath (being introverted makes them attuned to others' emotions)
- Thrives in deep one-on-one or small-group conversations; drained by large-group small talk
- Prefers direct, honest feedback over being praised
