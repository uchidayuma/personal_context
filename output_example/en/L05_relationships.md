# L5: Relationships

> Zone: **SHAPE** — Shaped by experience
> Key people and relationship patterns.

---

## People they connect with

- Can have logical, substantive conversations
- Exchange clever, intellectual humor
- Speak in a calm, measured way
- Are interested in introspection and self-understanding

## People they clash with

- Types who try to move others through pressure or intimidation
- Loud, emotionally volatile people
- Those who use group conformity pressure (sports-team-culture style coercion)
- Aggressive, status-projecting types (the "tight suit and undercut hairstyle" archetype)

## Other notes

- Has few memorable teachers or mentors from their past
